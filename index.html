What Is Parkour?

Have you ever seen a people jumping from one roof to another in movies? It is a sport - Parkour. It can play either male or female. The main concept of this sport is to pass the obstacles without touching the lines.

The game

It can play anywhere. Mostly it plays in the places that have obstacles. You don't need any equipments to play this sport

The Rules

  • If the athelete steps over the boundary lines, they will be disqualified
  • Athelets must avoid stepping on the sensitve aera such as (Church, Historical Places..)
  • Time is measured with a timekeeping system measuring in 1/1000th of a second

Major Sports Event

Parkour world competitions is hold every year. It holds in a places like Italy, Belgium and places that have many obstacles.
